Project-1

Overview

Project-1 is QUADRAID INDUSTRIES' flagship water purification system, engineered to meet the most demanding industrial and residential water quality standards. Combining cutting-edge molecular membrane technology with an energy-optimised design, Project-1 delivers superior filtration at a fraction of the operational cost of traditional systems.

Developed after years of rigorous R&D, Project-1 sets a new benchmark in clean water solutions — removing up to 99.99% of contaminants and pathogens while maintaining consistent high-volume throughput even in the harshest environments.

Key Features

Molecular Membrane Filtration

Our proprietary nano-scale membrane filters particles as small as 0.001 microns, providing pharmaceutical-grade water purity for both industrial and residential use.

Energy-Efficient Design

Project-1 consumes up to 40% less energy compared to conventional reverse osmosis systems, significantly reducing operational costs and carbon footprint.

High-Volume Throughput

Capable of processing up to 50,000 litres per hour, Project-1 scales effortlessly from small residential installations to large-scale industrial deployments.

Self-Cleaning Mechanism

An automated backwash and self-cleaning cycle minimises manual maintenance requirements, ensuring continuous uptime and membrane longevity.

Smart Monitoring

Integrated IoT sensors provide real-time water quality analytics, remote diagnostics, and predictive maintenance alerts via our cloud-connected dashboard.

Modular Architecture

The modular design allows for rapid on-site installation, easy capacity expansion, and simplified component replacement — minimising downtime during upgrades.
