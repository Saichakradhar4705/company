Project-2

Overview

Project-2 is QUADRAID INDUSTRIES' next-generation residential and light-commercial water purification system. Designed for compactness without compromise, it delivers the same industrial-grade water purity that QUADRAID is known for — packaged in a sleek, space-saving unit ideal for homes, offices, and small-scale commercial installations.

Built on our proprietary multi-stage nano-filtration technology, Project-2 removes up to 99.97% of contaminants, including heavy metals, microplastics, bacteria, and dissolved solids, producing clean, great-tasting water at the turn of a tap.

Key Features

Multi-Stage Nano-Filtration

A four-stage purification process — sediment pre-filter, activated carbon block, nano-membrane, and post-carbon polish — ensures consistently pure output water.

Compact Form Factor

Designed to fit under standard kitchen counters or in tight utility spaces, Project-2 occupies less than 30% of the footprint of conventional RO systems.

Zero Waste Technology

Unlike traditional RO systems that waste up to 3 litres for every litre purified, Project-2's closed-loop design achieves a 1:1 purification ratio, conserving water at every step.

Smart Filter Monitoring

An integrated digital display and companion mobile app track filter life, water quality metrics, and send maintenance reminders directly to your phone.

Tool-Free Installation

Plug-and-purify setup — no plumber required. Project-2 connects to any standard tap in under 15 minutes with our colour-coded quick-connect fittings.

Long-Life Membranes

Our durable nano-polymer membranes are rated for up to 5 years of continuous use before replacement, drastically reducing the ongoing cost of ownership.
